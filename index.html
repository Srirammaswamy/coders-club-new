About

About

In a culture where everything is mediated through computers, it becomes mandatory to be literate in the ways that technology works. One way through which this can be achieved is by practicing programming.Coders Club aims to establish this programming culture on TCE campus, reaching every student who are passionate about coding. It also aims at building good programmers with great programming habits. The club’s motto is to Think-Code-Repeat.

Motivation for the Club

The seed of the club was shown by the Head of the Department with an objective of improving the core placements of CSE students. It was then equipped by the final year students under the guidance of faculty memberswho wanted to contribute their knowledge for the benefit of upcoming students and allow them to explore the world of computer science. They are driven by their passion for coding and hunger to learn.

Objectives

1. To teach basics of programming as well as competitive programming to the students
2. To inculcate competitive coding practice and to encourage competitive programming participation in the events like ACM ICPC, CodeChef challenges.
3. To increase the number of students in leaderboard of coding sites like Hackerearth, Hackerrank.
4. To conduct regular coding challenges within the college.

Monthly Activities

● To conduct club meeting twice a month andsolve selective challenging problems in the coding sites/contest.
● To host new problems in the web page which has to be solved within next meeting.
● Invite Alumni and discuss the challenges in competitive programming contests through workshops.
